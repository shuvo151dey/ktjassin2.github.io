About Fest

Kshitij also called KTJ, is the annual techno-management festival of one of India’s premier technical institutions, the IIT Kharagpur.Kshitij’s journey started in 2004, from then its aim is to provide the technically inclined youth’s generation a national-level platform,where they can show their talents and expertise and would be recognized and rewarded.
